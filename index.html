Who Can Apply
Eligibility

This program is open to two groups within the BRYC community.

🎓

Current Fellows (Grades 8–12)

Active BRYC Fellows with at least 60% program attendance are eligible. Guardians may apply on behalf of their student.

⭐

BRYC Alumni

All BRYC alumni are eligible regardless of graduation year. No engagement requirement applies to alumni applicants.

ℹ️ How This Differs from the Basic Needs Grant

This financial support cycle operates differently from our standard Basic Needs Grant. We will not be collecting demographic data as part of this application, and participation does not obligate recipients to provide quotes or serve as program representatives. This is direct, flexible support for our community — plain and simple.

Review Process
1
Categorization
Review all applications and confirm the need category (L1/L2/L3) based on the applicant's dropdown selection and written description. Adjust the category if needed.
2
Calculate Totals
Add up all L3 requests, all L2 requests, and all L1 requests. Use the Summary tab to see totals by tier.
3
Allocation Within Each Tier
If under budget: Fund all requests at the requested amount (up to the tier maximum).

If over budget: Team uses discretion. Options include pro-rating all requests proportionally, prioritizing by urgency/severity within the tier, capping individual amounts to fund more people, or a combination of the above.
4
Verify Eligibility
For current Fellows: confirm the 60% attendance requirement is met by cross-checking the fall and spring attendance sheets before finalizing awards.
5
Notify Applicants
Send decision emails. Include pickup instructions for approved requests.
